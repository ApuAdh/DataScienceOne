Let's look at just 1989, the launch of Billboard's "Hot Rap Singles" chart

In 1989, regional independent labels dominated with superstar signings: Young MC and the Pharcyde are signed to Deliciouus Vinyl. Biz Markie (Just a friend), Big Daddy Kane (Smooth Operator), and Marley Marl are all on Cold Chillin'. Ruthless, Eazy E's label, has N.W.A. and the D.O.C. on its roster. Jive is crushing it with a roster that includes Boogie Down Productions, DJ Jazzy Jeff and the Fresh Prince, and Too $hort. And Tommy Boy preciently released both De La Soul and Queen Latifah's first albums.
